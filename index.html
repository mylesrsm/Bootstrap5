Debut or Die

데뷔 못 하면 죽는 병 걸림

Introduction

Debut or Die (데뷔 못 하면 죽는 병 걸림) is a Korean web fiction series by DS.Back that tells about the debut project of ‘Park Moondae’, who was challenged to become an idol due to the threat of sudden death.

Summary

After a night of drinking, aspiring civil servant Ryu Gunwoo passes out, only to awaken three years in the past in the body of a young man named Park Moondae. Before he can even assess the situation, he's given a mission: Debut or Die! Now, Moondae has one year to debut as an idol or face his doom. Luckily, he's able to snag a spot on the famous survival program "Idol Inc.," but with his skills so low and the competition so high, does Moondae stand a chance at becoming a shining star?

TeSTAR (테스타)

TeSTAR (테스타) is a 7-member multinational boy group from South Korea. The group was formed through the K-pop audition survival show Relisted! Idol Incorporated and debuted on June 18th, 202x. The final voting ranking was Park Moondae, Cha Eugene, Seon Ahyeon, Kim Raebin, Ryu Chungwoo, Lee Sejin B, and Lee Sejin A in order of first to seventh place. In the original timeline, the debuting group was called STier.

VTIC

VTIC is a popular male idol group under LeTi Entertainment. They are South Korea's most popular boygroup in this era and often become TeSTAR's competitor in terms of sales and streaming.
